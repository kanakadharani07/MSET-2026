MSET History

The School of Engineering and Technology (SET) at DSU has a proud tradition of hosting impactful international conferences.

Our previous edition, MSET 2025, saw an incredible turnout of over 500 participants from across the globe. Keynotes from industry leaders and high-quality paper presentations marked the success of the event.

  • MSET 2025: Held in April 2025. Focused on bridging mechanical, electrical, and computer science domains.

We are building upon the legacy of MSET 2025 to make MSET 2026 an even more enriching experience for all attendees.
