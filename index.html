About Us

The Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University (DSU) has been established under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019, located in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India. Uniqueness of DSU lies in its multi-disciplinary nature in offering a wide range of academic programmes encompassing medicine and engineering. Our motto is "education for the real world" with dedication and commitment towards nurturing the future generation. Green ambience with state-of-the-art infrastructure along with top-class faculty aims to serve the need of national and international students.

About School of Engineering and Technology

School of Engineering and Technology (SET) in DSU is a beacon of innovation and academic excellence, offering cutting-edge programs designed to shape the next generation of engineering leaders. SET has a range of 12 departments that include Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Agriculture Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Biotechnology, Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence and Date Structure, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Cyber Security and Internet of Things. With a strong emphasis on practical learning and research, SET equips students with the technical expertise, critical thinking abilities, and hands-on experience needed to excel in a competitive, technology-driven world. SET boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced Faculty who are dedicated to mentoring students and fostering a culture of academic rigor and innovation. State-of-the-art laboratories, well-equipped workshops, and modern computing facilities ensure that students have access to the latest tools and technologies to enhance their learning experience. In addition to academics, SET emphasizes holistic development by encouraging participation in technical clubs, competitions, and industry collaborations.

The Conference MSET2026!

📅 September 24 & 25, 2026   |   📍 Trichy, Tamil Nadu

Join us at MSET2026 in Trichy, Tamil Nadu. This event focuses on "SECOND INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON MULTIDISCIPLINARY SOLUTIONS IN ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY."

School of Engineering and Technology (SET) proudly hosts a two-day International Conference on Multidisciplinary Solutions in Engineering and Technology, MSET 2026, on 24th and 25th September 2026. Building on the resounding success of MSET 2025, this edition brings together global academics, researchers, industry professionals, and students to exchange cutting-edge ideas.

This conference is dedicated to addressing complex challenges through innovative, multidisciplinary approaches that bridge diverse fields of engineering and technology. Experts converge from domains including mechanical, electrical, computer science, electronics, science, humanities, and emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, machine learning, and IoT.

Key highlights of MSET 2026 include keynote speeches by renowned international and national experts, technical paper presentations, poster sessions, and panel discussions. Participants gain valuable insights into global trends, groundbreaking research, and practical applications shaping the future of engineering and technology.

Organized by SET at DSU, this event reaffirms the institution's commitment to advancing knowledge, fostering innovation, and promoting global academic and industrial collaboration.

Thematic Sessions

Technological Advancements & Challenges
Explore the opportunities and challenges posed by AI, automation, and IoT in transforming industries while addressing growing cybersecurity threats that require robust data protection and privacy solutions.
Food Science & Biotechnology in Agriculture
Development of genetically modified crops, sustainable farming practices, biotechnology in pest and disease control, improving crop yields and quality.
Integrated Approaches in Life Science
Interdisciplinary research in biology and medicine, advances in genomics and proteomics, innovative approaches to disease treatment, collaboration between academic and industry researchers.
Sustainability & Climate Change
Discuss how innovations in renewable energy, green technologies, and circular economies can mitigate climate change impacts such as rising temperatures, extreme weather events, and their effects on agriculture, water resources, and health.
Collaborative Research in Pure and Applied Mathematics
Advancements in theoretical mathematics, applications of mathematics in various industries, cross-disciplinary collaborations, impact of mathematical research on technology and society.
Healthcare Disparities & Aging Population
Addressing gaps in healthcare access in resource-limited regions, meeting the needs of the aging population, technological solutions like telemedicine and AI diagnostics, policy initiatives for equitable healthcare.
Urbanization & Resource Management
Delve into the pressures of rapid urbanization on water resources, exploring sustainable water management solutions for cities facing scarcity, pollution, and increased demand.
Globalization & Economic Inequality
Examine how globalization fosters innovation but also exacerbates economic inequality, and discuss policies to ensure a more equitable distribution of economic benefits.
📚 Education Access & Social Stability
Focus on the role of education in driving societal change, addressing the impacts of unequal access to quality education on poverty, unemployment, and social unrest.
Wellness Focus & Global Health Crises
Analyze the increasing focus on health and wellness, while addressing the ongoing global health crises, including pandemics and chronic diseases, particularly in underserved regions.
Migration & Geopolitical Instability
Investigate the political and social challenges associated with migration driven by conflict, economic instability, and climate change, and explore solutions for integrating displaced populations.
Incident Response and Threat Hunting
Strategies for effective incident response, techniques for threat detection and hunting, tools and technologies for cybersecurity, collaboration and information sharing.
Digital humanities, pedagogy & softskills
Addressing gaps in healthcare access in resource-limited regions, meeting the needs of the aging population, technological solutions like telemedicine and AI diagnostics, policy initiatives for equitable healthcare.

Publishing Details

Conference Proceedings

Abstracts of all accepted and presented papers will be published in the conference proceedings, which will be assigned an ISBN (International Standard Book Number). This ensures that the work is formally documented and accessible.

Extended Papers in Journals

Authors of selected papers will have the opportunity to extend their work and submit it for consideration in Scopus-indexed or UGC Care-listed journals. However, acceptance will depend on:

  • Scope: The paper must align with the journal's thematic focus.
  • Quality: The paper must meet the journal's academic and technical standards.
  • Financial Requirements: Authors may need to pay additional publication fees, if applicable, as per the journal's policies.
